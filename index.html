VinylHawk

VinylHawk is a dynamic vinyl pricing engine. We analyse popular marketplaces for your current listings, and use Machine Learning to predict the best prices to help sellers generate more sales.

How It Works

Being records collectors ourselves, we realized that adjusting prices for hundreds or thousands of items, while keeping track of the supply/demand in the marketplace, and while maximizing your own profit margins is a task that takes time and energy. RecordHawk aims to help you do this. Our algorithms keep track of the supply and demand per item, and offer pricing suggestions in a CSV, so that your items are seen consistently on top of Discogs marketplace listings, ultimately leading to more sales on your records. It's completely upto you if you decide to adjust prices by uploading the CSV to Discogs.
